About U-Ed and CLCGI

U-ED is a portal that connects public and private practitioners to a wide array of courses being offered by our Partner Universities.

U-ED does not administer academic programs nor confer degrees – these are carried out by its partner universities including the University of Makati (UMak), Philippine Christian University (PCU), and Isabela State University (ISU), among others.

The CLCGI is a service organization that provides continuing learning through capacity-enhancement programs in the areas of leadership communication in relation to governance. CLCGI also goes into consulting services in Leadership, Communication and Governance Research and Publications.
